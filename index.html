About


I am a master student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Department of Statistics and Data Science , where I work with Prof. Yifang Ma. I received my B.S. degree at SUSTech Mathematics Department in Jul. 2020. My current research focuses on the Computational Social Science and Data Science, especially Career development and performance in different career stages and the relations to gender, status and social network connections.
